How I Work

Every project follows a simple path to ensure the final design fits your goals perfectly.

Discovery

I begin by learning about your product, your users, and your goals. This helps me understand the problem before designing the solution.

Wireframes and Flows

I create simple wireframes and user flows that show how people will move through your product.

Visual Design

Once the structure is clear, I design polished screens in Figma. Colors, fonts, and layouts are set to match your brand.

Prototypes

I turn static designs into interactive prototypes to simulate the real experience.

Handoff

I provide developers with everything they need—assets, specs, and documentation.
